Frequently Asked Questions

What is SkillBarter?

SkillBarter is a platform where students can showcase their skills, connect with peers, and exchange knowledge by offering what they know and learning what they want.

How do I add my profile?

Click on the "Add Profile" button in the top navigation bar and fill in your details, skills you offer, and the skills you're looking to learn.

Is SkillBarter free to use?

Yes! The platform is completely free for students and young professionals looking to learn and grow.

Can I connect with any user?

Yes. Browse through profiles and click "Connect" to express interest. The other user can then respond to your request.

How do I edit or update my profile?

Head over to "My Profile" and click the "Edit Profile" button to update your information or change skills.
